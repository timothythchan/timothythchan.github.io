A statically-generated personal site built with Astro 5 and TypeScript. Features an interactive Three.js background with 5000 particles and rotating wireframe torus geometries that respond to mouse movement. Uses Astro's Content Collections for Markdown-driven blog posts and project entries, View Transitions for smooth page navigation, and a carefully crafted dark colour scheme.
